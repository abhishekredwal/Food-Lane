ORIGINAL

Where LA comes out to play

Bar Nineteen12 offers an indoor bar and a beautiful terrace overlooking the gardens and towering palms of Beverly Hills. This is a must for signature cocktails and spectacular views.

THE VIEW

This is the perfect, romantic spot to catch a brilliant Beverly Hills sunset and sip a cocktail before – and after – supper in the Polo Lounge.
